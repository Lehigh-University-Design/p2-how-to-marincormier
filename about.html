Brown Butter M&M Cookies

About the Recipe

My mom is a fantastic baker and bakes something for almost every occasion, but the baked good she has been famous for making are these M&M cookies. This recipe started out as a classic, simply the recipe right on the bag of M&Ms. It has been baked for every Cormier Family occasion. Over time, this family favorite evolved into a more complex recipe by browning the butter. The browned butter adds a deeper, caramelized flavor everyone loves.
