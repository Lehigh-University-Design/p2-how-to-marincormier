Brown Butter M&M Cookies

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. of baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp of flaky sea salt
  • 16 tbsp. (2 sticks, 8 oz.) of unsalted butter, one stick melted until brown
  • 1 cup + 2 tbsp. of dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla (vanilla bean paste is reccommended)
  • 2 1/2 cups of M&Ms
